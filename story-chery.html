Chery website & design system

Issues

The first task was to find an approach that would allow the project and all services to be launched in an extremely short time. The second challenge was to overcome the stereotypes and prejudices of customers about the Chinese cars, allowing the brand to compete with the world's leading manufacturers in digital space.

Goals

Focusing on the client's business objectives and analyzing consumer feedback, we were looking for a solution that would work to increase sales and user satisfaction at the same time. We needed to classify functional elements and content items in order to provide targeted access scenarios for different user groups.

New digital look

To turn the tide on brand perception, we proposed a radical change in the use of corporate identity, effectively rethinking of core identity for digital environments in the local market. We tried to give the brand a quiet luxury typical of the premium segment.

We decided to apply a strict black and white style, a calm tone that meets the ideas of simplicity and purity. For example, the corporate red, which previously prevailed, received a modest but important role as a signal color for interactive elements.
